WORK ABOUT

Designing a building is hard work.

But we do it with humor.

It all started in 2004, with two guys going out for a beer after a usual day of sketching and mouse-clicking. Realizing all work and no play @work isn't cutting it anymore, they started to make plans and soon teamed up with like-minded individuals, architects and ping-pong players nevertheless.

Since then, our do-it-together studio changed shapes and locations, yet we managed to produce an array of unique projects, each of them bringing new challenges and leaving us with technical know-how. Together with some of the best local engineers and general contractors, we strive to add value to each project we undertake, while also keeping track of expenditures. Our clients trust us with that.

The year 2012 brought us our first hospitality project, and after that others followed. We put our clients in high regard, so we work with them closely to ensure a transparent process. We wish we could say soothing process, however - since some of them return with new projects in mind - we must be doing something right.

So, if you like hour-long meetings, eating pizza over drawings and visiting a construction site when it's rainy, drop us a line, we'd love to work with you.
